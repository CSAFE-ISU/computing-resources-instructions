Chapter 5 The CSAFE RStudio Servers

CSAFE has two RStudio Servers that are operated by CSSM:

  1. https://reiss.csafe.iastate.edu/
  2. https://locard.csafe.iastate.edu/

*** Email cssm_it@iastate.edu for help

5.1 Connect to an RStudio Server

  1. If you are off campus, connect to the ISU VPN
  2. Go to the website https://reiss.csafe.iastate.edu/ or https://locard.csafe.iastate.edu/
  3. Log in with your ISU net-id and password. If you are an undergraduate student, use your CSAFE net-id.
